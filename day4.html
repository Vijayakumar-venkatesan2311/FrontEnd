Jasprit Bumrah World record

Jasprit Bumrah becomes first Indian bowler to achieve No.1 rankings in all three formats of the game. Star Indian pacer Jasprit Bumrah created history on Wednesday (February 7) by becoming the first bowler in the world to achieve the No. 1 ranking in all three formats of the game at least once in his career.
